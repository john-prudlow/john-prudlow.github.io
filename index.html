Who I Am

Greetings! My name is John. I’m a savvy web developer with 20 plus years of industry experience creating professional websites for clients. I started creating websites as a hobby back in the mid 90’s and have enjoyed programming and publishing them to the world ever since.

I am proficient in WordPress custom website development and maintenance using PHP, HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Javascript, jQuery, ACF, and other tools. I build well structured, maintainable, responsive websites and emails using best practices. I am also proficient in graphic and web design using the Adobe Creative Suite programs. I have several years of experience using numerous CRM and email marketing systems. I have extensive experience setting up hosting for websites, website security, and ongoing maintenance and management.

In addition to building websites, I enjoy playing guitar, running, swimming, configuring and fixing electronics, listening to music, watching movies and sports, and spending time with my family.
